FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Find answers to common questions about our real estate services and processes.

How do I start the property buying process?

Start by scheduling a free consultation with one of our agents. We will discuss your budget, preferences, and timeline. Then we will curate a list of properties that match your criteria and arrange viewings at your convenience.

What fees are involved in selling a property?

Our commission rates are competitive and transparent. We charge a standard percentage of the final sale price, which covers marketing, staging, photography, and full transaction management. No hidden fees guaranteed.

How long does the property buying process take?

The timeline varies based on market conditions and your specific needs. On average, from initial search to closing, the process takes 30-60 days. We work diligently to expedite every step while ensuring thorough due diligence.

Do you offer property management services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive property management services including tenant screening, rent collection, maintenance coordination, and regular property inspections. We handle everything so you can enjoy passive income stress-free.

Can I invest in real estate with a limited budget?

Absolutely! We offer various investment options including fractional ownership, REITs, and starter properties. Our investment consultants will help you find opportunities that align with your budget and financial goals.

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve major metropolitan areas across California, New York, Texas, and Illinois. Our network is continuously expanding. Contact us to confirm coverage in your specific area of interest.
